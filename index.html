THE SPLITS

Push

A push workout targets chest, shoulders, and triceps. It typically includes exercises like bench press, overhead press, and tricep dips. The routine focuses on pushing movements with 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps. Start with a warm-up and end with a cool-down for balanced muscle growth and recovery.

Pull

A pull workout targets the back and biceps. It typically includes exercises like pull-ups, rows, and bicep curls. The routine focuses on pulling movements with 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps. Start with a warm-up and end with a cool-down for balanced muscle growth and recovery.

Legs

A leg workout targets the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. It typically includes exercises like squats, lunges, leg presses, and calf raises. The routine focuses on lower body strength with 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps. Start with a warm-up and end with a cool-down for balanced muscle development and recovery.
